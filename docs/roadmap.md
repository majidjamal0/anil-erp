# Product roadmap

## Sprint 1 — Foundation

- Monorepo, containerized local runtime, CI, health checks, localization baseline
- Users, UUID-compatible RBAC, branches, warehouses, settings, audit log schemas

## Sprint 2 — Catalog and inventory

- Product variants, categories, units, barcodes, stock ledger, transfers and counts

## Sprint 3 — CRM and sales

- Customers, pricing, orders, returns, promotions, point-of-sale workflows

## Sprint 4 — Finance

- Chart of accounts, journals, receivables/payables, fixed-precision monetary reporting

## Sprint 5 — People and production

- Employees, attendance and payroll integration; bills of material, work orders, costing

## Sprint 6 — Intelligence and hardening

- Cross-domain reports, dashboards, exports, performance, disaster recovery and security review
